Our Story

Reed Family Racing was founded in 2020 by Tommy and Mallory Reed and is based out of Fort Oglethorpe, Georgia. What began as a family racing effort with their son, TW Reed, competing in go-karts has grown into a multi-driver team entering its seventh season of competition in 2026. Since its inception, Reed Family Racing has competed across multiple divisions and tracks throughout the Southeast while building a reputation for hard work, perseverance, and family-centered racing. Originally starting in dirt go-karts, the team has evolved over the years with multiple drivers representing RFR in competition. Today, the organization competes in Bandolero racing while continuing to grow both on and off the racetrack. From long nights in the shop to race weekends at tracks across the region, Reed Family Racing remains focused on developing drivers, building partnerships, and creating lasting memories as a family-owned motorsports team.
