TW Reed's racing journey began in 2021 at just eight years old when he climbed behind the wheel of a dirt kart for the first time at Dawgwood Speedway in Chatsworth, Georgia. Like many young racers, he entered the sport with plenty of determination and a willingness to learn. Despite being a rookie, TW quickly showed promise by finishing third in the highly competitive Georgia Burris Championship Series during his first season of competition.

In 2022, the hard work began to pay off. TW earned his first career victory at Dawgwood Speedway, a milestone moment that marked the beginning of what would become a successful racing career. He continued to gain experience throughout the season and finished fourth in the championship standings, building a foundation for future success.

The 2023 season proved to be a breakout year. TW opened the season in dramatic fashion by capturing the prestigious Dandridge Dash at Godspeed Raceway. In one of the most memorable moments of his young career, he made a last-lap pass that resulted in a thrilling photo-finish victory. The win sparked an incredible run of success, becoming the first of four consecutive victories. By season's end, TW had won seven of the first nine races he entered, establishing himself as one of the region's top young kart racers.

After several seasons of growth and consistent performance, TW reached a major milestone in 2024 by capturing his first track championship at Dawgwood Speedway. Looking for a new challenge, TW made the transition from dirt karts to Bandolero racing in 2025. From a rookie kart racer to a championship-winning driver, TW Reed's racing story continues to be written one lap at a time.
