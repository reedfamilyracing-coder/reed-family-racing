Logan Conner began his racing career in dirt go-karts and quickly developed into a competitive young driver. In his rookie season, Logan showed steady growth while finishing fourth in points at both Dawgwood Speedway and Ashway Speedway.

His second season proved to be a breakout year. Logan captured eight wins and finished second in points at Dawgwood Speedway, establishing himself as a strong competitor with the ability to run up front on race night.

With 12 wins across two seasons in dirt karts, Logan has continued to build confidence, experience, and racecraft. Now competing with Reed Family Racing in Bandolero racing, Logan is taking the next step in his racing journey.
