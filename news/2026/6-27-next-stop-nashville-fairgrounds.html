Next Stop: Nashville Fairgrounds

Reed Family Racing prepares for the return to the Nashville Fairgrounds

Share This Story

Track: Nashville Fairgrounds (0.25 Mile Oval)

Opened: 1904

Pit Gates Open: 9:30 AM

Practice Begins: 1:00 PM

Grandstands Open:3:00 PM

Racing Begins:5:00 PM

Grandstands: $10 Adults

Kids 11 and Under:Free

Reed Family Racing heads back to Nashville Fairgrounds Speedway this weekend for another opportunity at one of the most historic short tracks in the country. Any time you get the chance to race at the Fairgrounds, it feels special. The lights, the grandstands, the history, and the atmosphere all make it a place that feels bigger than just another race on the schedule.

For Logan Conner and the #52 team, this weekend also brings a little unfinished business.

The last time Logan raced at Nashville, the night ended with a hard hit against the front stretch wall. It was one of those moments that every race team hopes to avoid, but one that every driver has to learn from. Moving into Bandolero racing has brought a lot of new challenges, and Nashville made sure to remind us just how tough the learning curve can be. But racing has never been about avoiding the hard days. It is about what you do after them.

Since that last trip to the Fairgrounds, Logan has continued to gain seat time, confidence, and experience behind the wheel. Every lap has mattered. Every practice session, every adjustment, and every race has been part of the process as he continues learning what these cars need and how to get more comfortable on asphalt.

This weekend is not about looking back at the wreck. It is about going back with a better understanding, a little more confidence, and the same determination that has carried Logan through every step of his racing career.

The goal is simple: unload, improve every time the car hits the track, and bring the #52 home in one piece. If we can do that, it will be another step forward for Logan and Reed Family Racing. We’re excited to get back under the lights at Nashville Fairgrounds Speedway and see what this weekend has in store.
